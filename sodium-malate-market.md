<p><p>The sodium malate market includes two main types: monosodium malate and disodium malate. Monosodium malate is a compound where only one sodium atom is linked to the malic acid molecule, while disodium malate contains two sodium atoms attached to the malic acid molecule. Both types are commonly used in the food and beverage industry as acidulants and flavor enhancers. Monosodium malate is often preferred for its milder taste, while disodium malate is known for its stronger acidic properties. These variants cater to different preferences and applications within the market.</p></p>
