<p><p>Heated dog houses can be categorized into two main types: central heater and heating pad market. In the central heater market, the dog houses are equipped with a system that uses an internal heating unit to provide warmth. This type of heated dog house is more suitable for colder climates and larger dogs. On the other hand, the heating pad market offers dog houses with built-in heating pads, which provide localized warmth for the dog. This type is often preferred for smaller dogs and milder climates where less heat is necessary.</p></p>
