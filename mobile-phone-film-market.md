Furthermore, the introduction of anti-glare, anti-fingerprint, and anti-bacterial mobile phone films has also gained traction in the market.</p><p>Another emerging trend is the availability of customized mobile phone films. <p><ul><li>Plastic Film</li><li>Glass Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile phone film market consists of two main types: plastic film and glass film. Plastic film refers to a thin layer made of flexible plastic material that is applied onto the mobile phone screen. It offers protection against scratches and minor impacts. On the other hand, glass film is a thin layer of tempered glass that provides enhanced protection against scratches, impacts, and even shattering of the phone screen. Both types of films aim to safeguard the mobile phone screen from daily wear and tear, but glass film offers a sturdier and more durable option compared to plastic film.</p></p>
