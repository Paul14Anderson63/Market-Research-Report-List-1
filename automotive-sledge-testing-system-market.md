<p><p>Automotive sledge testing systems are utilized to simulate real-life crash scenarios and evaluate the performance of vehicles and safety equipment. Decelerator sledge testing systems involve restraining a vehicle and accelerating it, then suddenly stopping it to measure the impact and assess occupant safety. On the other hand, catapult sledge testing systems are designed to launch a vehicle forward, replicating accidents such as rollovers. Both types of testing systems are crucial in enhancing automotive safety standards and providing valuable data to manufacturers for the development of safer vehicles.</p></p>
