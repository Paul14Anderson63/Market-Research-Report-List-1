<p><p>A reflective encoder is an optical encoder that uses a light-emitting diode (LED) or a laser diode to emit light onto a code disk, which then reflects back through a sensor. The sensor reads the reflected light and converts it into position or speed data. These encoders are widely used in various industries such as manufacturing, robotics, aerospace, and healthcare for accurate measurement and control of position, speed, and direction.</p><p>The future outlook for the reflective encoder market looks promising.
