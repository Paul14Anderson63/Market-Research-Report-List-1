<p><p>The Metal Processing Lubricant Market can be classified into three main types: Quenching, Cutting and Grinding, and Corrosion Preventives. Quenching lubricants assist in cooling down the heated metal during the hardening process. Cutting and Grinding lubricants are used to reduce friction and heat generation while machining metal. They enhance cutting performance and extend tool life. Corrosion Preventives protect metal surfaces from oxidation and corrosion, ensuring their longevity and durability. These lubricants play a crucial role in improving metal processing efficiency and maintaining the quality of finished products.</p></p>
