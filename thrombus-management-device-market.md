<p><p>The thrombus management device market comprises various types of devices used for treating blood clots. Neurovascular embolectomy devices are used to remove blood clots in the brain's blood vessels. Embolectomy balloon catheters are inserted into the affected blood vessel and inflated to remove the clot. Percutaneous thrombectomy devices mechanically extract the clot from the blood vessel. Catheter-guided thrombolysis devices deliver medication directly to the clot to dissolve it. These devices play a crucial role in managing thrombus, or blood clot, related conditions, promoting effective treatment outcomes.</p></p>
