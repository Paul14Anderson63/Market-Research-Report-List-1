<p><p>Cylinder vacuum cleaners come in two main types: bagged and bagless. Bagged vacuum cleaners use disposable bags to collect and store dirt and debris, providing a convenient way to dispose of collected waste. On the other hand, bagless vacuum cleaners collect dirt and debris in a reusable dust container, eliminating the need for disposable bags and reducing waste. Both types offer their own advantages, with bagged options generally being more hygienic and easier to clean, while bagless options offer cost savings by eliminating the need for purchasing replacement bags.</p></p>
