<p><p>Powder Metallurgy (PM) encompasses a range of processes used to produce metal components from fine powders. The market is divided into two main types: Ferrous and Non-ferrous. Ferrous PM primarily includes iron and iron-based alloys, which offer excellent strength and wear resistance suitable for automotive and industrial applications. Non-ferrous PM involves metals such as aluminum, copper, and titanium, offering lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and high-performance components used in aerospace, energy, and medical sectors. Both markets rely on powder compaction, sintering, and high-quality powder formulations to achieve desired properties and meet specific industry requirements.</p></p>
