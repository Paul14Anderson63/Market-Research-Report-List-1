<p><p>Carrageenan powder is a widely used ingredient in the food and beverage industry. There are three main types of carrageenan powder: Kappa Carrageenan, Iota Carrageenan, and Lambda Carrageenan. </p><p>Kappa Carrageenan is known for its strong gelling properties and is commonly used in dairy products, such as ice cream and chocolate milk. </p><p>Iota Carrageenan forms a soft gel and is often utilized in products like puddings, jellies, and toppings. </p><p>Lambda Carrageenan, on the other hand, acts as a thickening agent and stabilizer, making it ideal for use in salad dressings, sauces, and canned pet foods. Each type has unique properties that cater to different food applications.</p></p>
