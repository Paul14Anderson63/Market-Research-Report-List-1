<p><p>Axle Counter Cable market includes various types such as Twisted Pair Cables and Star-quad Cables. Twisted Pair Cables are composed of two insulated copper wires twisted together, reducing electromagnetic interference and crosstalk. They are commonly used in data and telecommunications applications. On the other hand, Star-quad Cables consist of four insulated conductors twisted together in pairs and then twisted again. This design enhances noise rejection and is suitable for high-quality audio and balanced line applications. These cable types cater to different needs, offering reliable transmission and reduced interference in their respective fields.</p></p>
