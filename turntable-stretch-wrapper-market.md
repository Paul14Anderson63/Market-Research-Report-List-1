<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The turntable stretch wrapper market consists of three main types: manual stretch wrappers, semiautomatic stretch wrappers, and automatic stretch wrappers. Manual stretch wrappers require the operator to control the wrapping process entirely, with limited automation. Semiautomatic stretch wrappers involve some automation, such as motorized film carriages, but still require operator intervention for load placement and cutting film. Automatic stretch wrappers are fully automated, operating without the need for operator involvement, utilizing technology like conveyors for load transfer and automatic film cutting systems.</p></p>
