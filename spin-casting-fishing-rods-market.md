<p><p>Spin casting fishing rods are divided into two market types: freshwater spinning rods and saltwater spinning rods. Freshwater spinning rods are specifically designed for fishing in freshwater environments such as rivers, lakes, and ponds. They are typically lightweight and sensitive, allowing for accurate casting and better control while targeting freshwater species like trout, bass, or panfish. On the other hand, saltwater spinning rods are built to withstand the harsh conditions of the ocean. They are generally more durable and resistant to corrosion, making them suitable for catching larger and tougher saltwater species such as tuna, marlin, or snapper.</p></p>
