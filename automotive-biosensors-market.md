Thermal biosensors are a type of automotive biosensor that measures temperature changes in the vehicle engine, exhaust system, or other components. Piezo-electric biosensors, on the other hand, utilize the piezoelectric effect to convert pressure into an electrical signal and are commonly used to measure tire pressure in vehicles. Both types of biosensors play a crucial role in ensuring vehicle performance, efficiency, and safety.</p></p>
