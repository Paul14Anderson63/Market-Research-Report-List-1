<p><p>Metal cutting fluids and oils are essential in lubricating and cooling processes during metal cutting operations. Water-based cutting fluids are composed of water mixed with additives that enhance lubrication and anti-corrosion properties. Cutting oils, on the other hand, are petroleum-based fluids, either mineral or synthetic, that offer high lubricity and heat transfer capabilities. Emulsions are a combination of oil and water, where the oil droplets are dispersed in water, providing a balance between lubrication and cooling effects. These market types cater to different industrial needs and preferences in metal cutting processes.</p></p>
