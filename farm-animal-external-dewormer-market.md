<p><p>The farm animal external dewormer market includes two main types: injection and unguent. Injection dewormers are administered directly into the bloodstream of the farm animals through a needle. This allows the medication to work internally, targeting internal parasites. On the other hand, unguent dewormers are applied topically on the animal's skin or coat. These dewormers are usually in the form of a paste or a gel and work externally by killing external parasites such as ticks, lice, and mites. Both types of dewormers play a crucial role in maintaining the health and well-being of farm animals.</p></p>
