<p><p>The long-lasting hair color market consists of two major types: permanent and semi-permanent. Permanent hair color is formulated with chemicals that penetrate the hair shaft, resulting in a lasting color that remains until new hair growth occurs. On the other hand, semi-permanent hair color coats the hair shaft with color that gradually fades over time, typically lasting for a few weeks. Both types provide different options for individuals seeking to change or enhance their hair color, permanent for a long-lasting effect and semi-permanent for a more temporary change.</p></p>
