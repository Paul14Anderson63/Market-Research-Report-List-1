<p><ul><li>Internal</li><li>External</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fixed-wing aircraft fuel tank market consists of two types: internal tanks and external tanks. Internal tanks are integrated into the aircraft structure and are located within the aircraft's fuselage or wings. They are primarily used for carrying the main fuel load. External tanks, on the other hand, are attached to the external surface of the aircraft. They are optional and used for extended range or specialized missions. Both types contribute to the overall fuel capacity of the aircraft, allowing for increased range and operational flexibility.</p></p>
