<p><p>Automobile safety glass, also known as laminated glass, is a type of glass designed to ensure the safety of occupants in a vehicle in the event of a collision or impact. <p><p>The automobile safety glass market comprises three main types: tempered glass, laminated glass, and others. Tempered glass is known for its strength and safety features, as it shatters into small, less harmful pieces upon impact. Laminated glass, on the other hand, consists of two or more layers that are bound together by an interlayer, providing enhanced protection against shattering. Lastly, the "others" category encompasses various glass types, such as acoustic glass and bullet-resistant glass, which offer specific functionalities to address different safety concerns in automobiles.</p></p>
