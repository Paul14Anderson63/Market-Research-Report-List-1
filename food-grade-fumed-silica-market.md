<p><p>Food Grade Fumed Silica market is categorized into various types based on its BET (Brunauer-Emmett-Teller) surface area. The types include BET 100-160, BET 160-210, BET 210-300, and Others. BET 100-160 indicates the surface area of the fumed silica falls between 100 and 160 m²/g. Similarly, BET 160-210 means the surface area ranges from 160 to 210 m²/g, and BET 210-300 indicates the surface area lies between 210 and 300 m²/g. The category "Others" includes fumed silica with surface areas outside these specified ranges. These classifications help differentiate fumed silica products according to their surface area properties in the food industry.</p></p>
