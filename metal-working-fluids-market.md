<p><p>Metal Working Fluids (MWFs) are specialized chemicals used in various metalworking processes to improve performance and extend the life of tools and machines. These fluids can be classified into four types: Metal Removal Fluids, used for shaping and cutting metals through processes like milling and drilling; Metal Treating Fluids, employed in heat treatment processes to enhance the characteristics of metal components; Metal Forming Fluids, used in processes like forging and stamping to facilitate shaping and reduce friction; and Metal Protecting Fluids, which provide corrosion resistance and protection during storage and transportation of metal parts.</p></p>
