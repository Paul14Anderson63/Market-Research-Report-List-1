<p><p>Sintered Nd-Fe-B magnets are available in two market types: axially magnetized and diametrically magnetized. Axially magnetized magnets have their magnetic field aligned along the axis of the magnet, providing strong magnetic force at the ends. On the other hand, diametrically magnetized magnets have their magnetic field aligned across the diameter, offering strong magnetic force on the sides. Both types are utilized in various applications, such as motors, generators, and magnetic separators, based on their specific alignment and magnetic field strength.</p></p>
