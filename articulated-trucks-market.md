<p><p>Articulated trucks are divided into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I articulated trucks have a separate engine and cab, allowing for better flexibility in maneuvering tight spaces and uneven terrains. These trucks are commonly used in industries such as construction and mining. On the other hand, Type II articulated trucks have an integrated design with the cab and engine together, providing a more compact and efficient option for transport. Type II trucks are often used in transportation sectors like logistics and delivery services.</p></p>
