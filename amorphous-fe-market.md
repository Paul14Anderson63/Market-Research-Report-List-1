<p><p>Amorphous Fe market comprises two types: Amorphous Ribbon and Nano-crystalline Ribbon. It finds extensive applications in transformers, magnetic sensors, and magnetic shielding. On the other hand, Nano-crystalline Ribbon refers to a Fe-based alloy that possesses small nanoscale crystalline grains, exhibiting improved soft magnetic properties. It is commonly used in high-frequency devices, such as inductors, filters, and power transformers.</p></p>
