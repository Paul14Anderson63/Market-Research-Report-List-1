<p><p>The electric bus charging infrastructure market consists of two main types: wired charging and wireless charging. Wired charging refers to the method of using physical cables to connect the bus to a charging station. It involves the installation of charging equipment and cables that deliver electricity to the bus. On the other hand, wireless charging eliminates the need for physical cables by utilizing electromagnetic fields to transfer energy from the charging infrastructure to the bus. This technology enables charging by simply parking the bus over a charging pad or plate.</p></p>
