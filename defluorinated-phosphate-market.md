<p><p>The defluorinated phosphate market is classified into two types: sinter market and melt market. In contrast, the melt market involves the production of defluorinated phosphate by melting raw materials at high temperatures, producing a more concentrated liquid form that is commonly used in the production of phosphoric acid or as a raw material in the chemical industry.</p></p>
