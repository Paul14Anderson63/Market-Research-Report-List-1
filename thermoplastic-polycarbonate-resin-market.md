<p><p>The thermoplastic polycarbonate resin market is divided into two main types: extrusion grade and molding grade. Extrusion grade is specifically formulated to be used in processes where the resin is heated and formed into a continuous shape, such as in the production of sheets, tubes, and profiles. Molding grade, on the other hand, is designed for use in injection molding processes, where the resin is melted and injected into a mold to create complex shapes and structures. These different grades cater to specific manufacturing needs and requirements.</p></p>
