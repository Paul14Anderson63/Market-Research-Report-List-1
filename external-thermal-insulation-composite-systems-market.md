<p><p>External Thermal Insulation Composite Systems (ETICS) are widely used to improve the energy efficiency and thermal performance of buildings. The market for ETICS can be divided into two main types: polymer-based and polymer-modified. Polymer-based systems utilize a polymer as the primary material in the insulation layer, providing excellent thermal resistance and durability. On the other hand, polymer-modified systems include a polymer additive in the insulation layer, enhancing its properties such as flexibility, adhesion, and impact resistance. Both types of ETICS play a crucial role in reducing energy consumption and enhancing the overall sustainability of buildings.</p></p>
