<p><p>Ceramic pressure sensors are categorized into two types: Simulation Output Signals and Digital Output Signals market. Simulation Output Signals refer to sensors that generate analog signals proportional to the applied pressure. These signals are typically represented by voltage or current. On the other hand, Digital Output Signals refer to sensors that produce digital signals, usually in the form of binary code (0s and 1s), to indicate pressure levels. These signals can be easily processed and transmitted by digital systems.</p></p>
