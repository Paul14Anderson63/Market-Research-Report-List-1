<p><p>Absorbable suture thread is a type of surgical suture that is designed to break down and be absorbed by the body over time. With an increasing number of surgical procedures being performed globally and a growing demand for advanced wound closure techniques, the market is expected to experience steady growth in the coming years. Manufacturers are focusing on developing innovative absorbable sutures with enhanced capabilities, such as improved tensile strength and prolonged absorption rates, to cater to the specific needs of different surgical procedures.</p><p>The current COVID-19 pandemic has also had a significant impact on the absorbable suture thread market.
