<p><strong>The Single-ply Roofing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PVC (polyvinyl chloride)</li><li>EPDM (rubber)</li><li>TPO (thermoplastic)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single-ply roofing system market consists of three main types: PVC, EPDM, and TPO. PVC (polyvinyl chloride) is a durable thermoplastic material known for its resistance to chemicals and UV rays. EPDM (rubber) is a flexible synthetic rubber membrane that provides excellent weather resistance and durability. TPO (thermoplastic olefin) is a blend of polypropylene and ethylene-propylene rubber, known for its energy efficiency and resistance to impact and punctures. These roofing systems offer various benefits and are commonly used in commercial and industrial buildings for their reliability and performance.</p></p>
