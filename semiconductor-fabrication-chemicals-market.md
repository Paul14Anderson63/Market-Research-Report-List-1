<p><p>Semiconductor fabrication chemicals are categorized into three types: gas chemicals, solid chemicals, and liquid chemicals. Gas chemicals are in gaseous form and are used for various processes in semiconductor fabrication, such as etching and deposition. Solid chemicals come in powder or solid form and are typically used for thin film depositions. Liquid chemicals are solutions or dispersions used for cleaning, wet etching, and polishing semiconductor surfaces. Each type of chemical plays a crucial role in different stages of semiconductor manufacturing, ensuring the precise and controlled fabrication of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
