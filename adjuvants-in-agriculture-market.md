<p><p>Adjuvants play a crucial role in agriculture by enhancing the effectiveness of pesticides and herbicides. Alkoxylates are adjuvants that improve the spreading properties and stability of agrochemicals, allowing for better coverage. Sulfonates act as wetting agents, reducing surface tension and facilitating the uniform distribution of pesticides. Organosilicones improve the penetration and absorption of pesticides into plant tissues, increasing their efficacy. Other adjuvants may include dispersants, buffering agents, or antifoaming agents, which provide additional benefits such as preventing clogging or improving solubility.</p></p>
