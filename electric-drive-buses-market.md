Hybrid Electric buses combine an internal combustion engine with an electric motor, reducing fuel consumption and emissions. Plug-in Hybrid Electric buses can be charged externally and also utilize an internal combustion engine. Battery Electric buses rely solely on electric power and need to be charged externally. Each type offers varying degrees of environmental sustainability and operational flexibility.</p></p>
