<p><p>Shipborne Air Early Warning (AEW) refers to the system used by naval forces to detect and track airborne threats such as enemy aircraft, missiles, or drones. Shipborne AEW systems are typically installed on warships and aircraft carriers to extend the surveillance range and coverage of the naval forces.</p><p>The Shipborne Air Early Warning (AEW) Market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 6.5% during the forecast period. Integration of various sensors such as radar, electronic warfare systems, and communication systems within a single platform is a notable trend in the market. This integration enables seamless communication and data sharing between naval assets, enhancing the overall effectiveness of the AEW system.</p><p>Additionally, the growing adoption of unmanned aerial systems (UAS) for maritime surveillance is also driving the market growth.
