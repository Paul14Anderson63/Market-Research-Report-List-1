<p><p>The Vehicle Backup Camera Modules market is segmented into CCD cameras and CMOS cameras. CCD cameras, or charge-coupled device cameras, use a sensor that converts light into electrons and then into a digital signal. They offer high-quality images with low noise, making them suitable for professional applications. On the other hand, CMOS cameras, or complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor cameras, use a different type of sensor technology. They are generally more affordable, consume less power, and provide faster data transfer rates. CMOS cameras are commonly used in consumer electronics and offer a good balance between cost and performance.</p></p>
