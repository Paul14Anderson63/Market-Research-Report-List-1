<p><p>The coffee pod market can be categorized into three types: soft coffee pods, hard coffee pods, and capsules. Soft coffee pods are made of filter paper and are typically used in pod machines that brew coffee. Hard coffee pods, on the other hand, are made of plastic or metal and are mainly used in single-serve coffee machines. Capsules are sealed containers that preserve the freshness of coffee grounds and are compatible with specific machines. These different types cater to the diverse preferences of consumers looking for convenient and efficient ways to enjoy their cup of coffee.</p></p>
