<p><p>The Manufacturing Industrial Casters market includes two types of casters: swivel casters and rigid casters. Swivel casters are designed to rotate 360 degrees, allowing for easy maneuverability and change in direction. They are commonly used in applications that require flexible movement, such as industrial equipment and furniture. On the other hand, rigid casters are fixed and do not rotate, offering stability and straight-line movement. These casters are typically utilized in applications where stability and precise control are crucial, such as heavy machinery and medical equipment.</p></p>
