<p><p>There are two types of wheel hub motors in the automobile industry: Inner-Rotor Motor and External-Rotor Motor. </p><p>The Inner-Rotor Motor is a type of wheel hub motor where the rotor is located inside the stator. This design provides a compact and lightweight solution, making it ideal for electric vehicles. It offers high torque and efficiency, making it suitable for various applications.</p><p>On the other hand, the External-Rotor Motor has its rotor located outside the stator. This design provides better cooling capabilities and allows for higher torque output. It is commonly used in larger vehicles or applications that require more demanding power output.</p></p>
