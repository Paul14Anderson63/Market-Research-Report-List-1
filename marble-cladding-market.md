<p><p>Marble cladding is widely used in the construction industry to enhance the aesthetic appeal of buildings. There are three main types of marble cladding in the market. Panel type refers to large marble panels that are installed onto the exterior walls of the building. Sheet type marble cladding involves small marble sheets that are used to cover the entire surface with joints between them. Brickwork type marble cladding imitates the appearance of a brick wall, with small marble pieces arranged in a brick pattern. Each type offers different design choices and installation methods to suit various architectural needs.</p></p>
