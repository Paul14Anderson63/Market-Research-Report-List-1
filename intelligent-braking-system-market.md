<p><p>The Intelligent Braking System (IBS) market consists of various types including Anti-lock Braking System (ABS), Electronic Braking System (EBS), and others. ABS is a safety feature that prevents wheels from locking up during sudden braking, ensuring stability and steering control. EBS further enhances the ABS by incorporating electronic controls to improve performance, responsiveness, and overall safety. Other types of IBS may include features such as brake force distribution, emergency brake assist, and collision avoidance systems, which adaptively adjust braking dynamics based on environmental conditions. These systems collectively aim to enhance vehicle safety and prevent accidents.</p></p>
