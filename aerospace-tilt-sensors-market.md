<p><p>Aerospace tilt sensors are used in the aviation industry to measure and detect the tilt or angular position of aircraft or spacecraft. There are two main types of tilt sensors in the market: analog and digital. Analog tilt sensors provide continuous output signals, representing the tilt angle as a voltage or current. On the other hand, digital tilt sensors provide discrete output signals, typically in the form of binary code, indicating the tilt angle in predefined increments. Both analog and digital tilt sensors play a crucial role in aerospace applications for monitoring and controlling the orientation of aircraft.</p></p>
