<p><strong>The Cool Roofs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PVC(Polyvinyl Chloride)</li><li>EPDM(Rubber)</li><li>TPO(Thermoplastic)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cool roofs are a type of roofing system designed to reflect sunlight and prevent heat absorption, thus reducing energy consumption and lowering cooling costs. PVC cool roofs are made of polyvinyl chloride materials, offering excellent heat resistance and durability. EPDM cool roofs use rubber materials known for their flexibility and weather resistance. TPO cool roofs are made of thermoplastic materials, combining the benefits of PVC and EPDM roofs. All these cool roof types contribute to energy efficiency by effectively reflecting solar heat and improving overall insulation.</p></p>
