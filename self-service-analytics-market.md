<p><p>The self-service analytics market consists of two main types: on-premises and on-cloud. On-premises analytics refer to data analysis conducted within a company's own infrastructure, utilizing their hardware and software systems. This setup provides full control but requires significant upfront investment. On the other hand, on-cloud analytics involve using cloud-based platforms and tools provided by third-party service providers. This option offers scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness, as companies pay for resources as needed. Both types enable users to independently access, explore, and analyze data to gain insights and make informed decisions.</p></p>
