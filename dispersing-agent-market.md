<p><p>Dispersing agents are chemicals widely used in various industries to improve the dispersion of solid particles in a liquid medium. One classification of dispersing agents is based on their molecular weight, which can be categorized into high-molecular-weight and low-molecular-weight dispersing agents. High-molecular-weight dispersing agents are composed of large-sized molecules, known as polymers, that offer better stability and efficient dispersion of particles. On the other hand, low-molecular-weight dispersing agents consist of smaller molecules and provide advantages such as fast and easy dispersion, enhanced compatibility with different systems, and improved viscosity control.</p></p>
