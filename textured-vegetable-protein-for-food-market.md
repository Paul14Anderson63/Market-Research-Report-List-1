<p><p>Textured Vegetable Protein (TVP) refers to a plant-based protein product derived from soybeans or other vegetables. In the food market, TVP is available in two main types: protein concentrate and protein isolate. Protein concentrate contains a higher amount of protein and retains more of the original nutritional value of the soybean or vegetable. On the other hand, protein isolate undergoes further processing to remove more of the carbohydrates and fats, resulting in a purer and more concentrated protein product. Both types are commonly used as meat substitutes in vegetarian and vegan diets.</p></p>
