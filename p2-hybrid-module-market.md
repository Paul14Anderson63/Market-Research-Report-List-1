<p><ul><li>P2 On-Axis Drive Module</li><li>P2 Off-Axis Drive Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The P2 Hybrid Module market consists of two main types: the P2 On-Axis Drive Module and the P2 Off-Axis Drive Module. The P2 On-Axis Drive Module employs a direct drive system, where the motor is directly coupled with the load, leading to efficient power transmission and higher torque density. On the other hand, the P2 Off-Axis Drive Module uses a belt or gear mechanism to transmit power between the motor and the load, offering flexibility in system design and allowing for compact installations in limited spaces. Both modules provide solutions for various applications, depending on specific requirements.</p></p>
