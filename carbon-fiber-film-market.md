<p><p>The carbon fiber film market can be classified into three types based on the structure of the film: single-walled, double-walled, and multi-walled. Single-walled carbon fiber film consists of a single layer of carbon atoms arranged in a hexagonal pattern. Double-walled carbon fiber film contains two layers of carbon atoms arranged in a hexagonal pattern, providing increased strength and durability. Multi-walled carbon fiber film has multiple layers of carbon atoms arranged in a hexagonal pattern, offering superior stiffness and mechanical properties compared to the other types. These variations in structure determine the performance and applications of carbon fiber films.</p></p>
