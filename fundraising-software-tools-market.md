<p><p>Fundraising software tools can be classified into two market types, namely cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based fundraising software operates on the internet, allowing users to access and utilize the software remotely through web browsers. It offers the benefit of flexibility, scalability, and automatic updates. On the other hand, on-premise fundraising software is installed directly on the user's servers or computers. It provides localized control, security, and customization but requires maintenance and IT support. Both options cater to different needs and preferences of organizations seeking fundraising solutions.</p></p>
