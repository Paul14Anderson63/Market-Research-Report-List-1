<p><p>The scanner software market includes two main types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise software is installed and operated directly on the user's local hardware or server, providing complete control over data and infrastructure but requiring maintenance and updates. Cloud-based software, on the other hand, is hosted and accessed through the internet, eliminating the need for local installations and allowing for scalability and remote access. It offers flexibility and cost-effectiveness, as the software provider handles updates and maintenance.</p></p>
