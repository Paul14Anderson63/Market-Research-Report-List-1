CDEA is a market type where external assistance is provided, but it has certain constraints imposed by the assisting countries. On the other hand, CMEA is a market system coordinated by a council where member countries provide mutual economic assistance to each other. Both market types involve external collaboration, but CDEA has restrictions, while CMEA is based on mutual support among member nations.</p></p>
