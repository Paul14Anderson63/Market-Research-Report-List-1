<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The food twin-screw extruder market consists of three types: parallel co-rotating, parallel counter-rotating, and conical. Parallel co-rotating extruders have two screws rotating in the same direction, providing high shear, mixing, and conveying capabilities. Parallel counter-rotating extruders have screws rotating in opposite directions, allowing precise control over temperature and shear. On the other hand, conical extruders have tapered screws that enable efficient conveying and high pressure buildup. Each type offers unique benefits and is suited for different food processing applications.</p></p>
