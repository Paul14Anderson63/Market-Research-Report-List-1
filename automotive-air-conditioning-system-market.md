<p><p>The automotive air-conditioning system market includes various types of systems, including the CCOT (Cycling Clutch Orifice Tube) system, the TXV (Thermal Expansion Valve) system, and others. The CCOT system is a common type that regulates refrigerant flow by cycling the compressor on and off. The TXV system, on the other hand, controls the refrigerant flow based on temperature and pressure, delivering a more precise and efficient cooling performance. Other types of systems in the market encompass alternative technologies, such as electronic control or hybrid systems, which aim to further improve energy efficiency and user comfort.</p></p>
