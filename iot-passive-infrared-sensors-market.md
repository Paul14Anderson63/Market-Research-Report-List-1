<p><p>The IoT Passive Infrared Sensors market includes three main types: wide-angle, curtain ceremony, and directional sensors. Wide-angle sensors monitor a large area, detecting movements within a broad range. Curtain ceremony sensors are designed to detect motion across a narrow path or specific zone, making them ideal for doorways or windows. Directional sensors are highly precise, identifying the direction of the movement.
