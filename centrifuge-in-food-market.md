<p><p>Centrifuge systems play a crucial role in the food market for various purposes. Vertical centrifuge is a type where the food mixture is spun vertically, allowing heavier components to settle at the bottom while lighter substances rise to the top, enabling separation. On the other hand, horizontal centrifuge operates by spinning the mixture horizontally, facilitating efficient separation based on density differences. Both types are used to clarify liquids, extract liquids from solids, and obtain desired fractions in the food industry, ensuring quality and purity of products.</p></p>
