This film is used for manufacturing pouches, bags, and wraps, which are lightweight, cost-effective, and offer excellent product visibility.</p><p>Furthermore, the automotive industry is witnessing a surge in demand for polypropylene embossed film due to its high heat resistance and chemical stability. It is used for surface protection, interior trim, and decorative applications in automobiles.</p><p>The market is also witnessing several trends that are expected to shape its future. Innovation in film manufacturing technologies, such as multilayer co-extrusion and nano-coating, is leading to the development of high-performance embossed films with enhanced properties.
