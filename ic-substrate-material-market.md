The expansion of 5G networks and the implementation of Internet of Things (IoT) devices are also driving the market growth. <p><p>The IC substrate material market comprises various types such as substrate resin, copper foil, insulation materials, drill, and others. Substrate resin refers to the material used for the base of an integrated circuit, providing mechanical support and electrical connectivity. Copper foil is the conductive layer that allows the flow of electricity in the circuit. Insulation materials are used to provide electrical insulation between different layers of the circuit. Drill refers to the process of creating holes in the circuit for component placement. Other materials may include additives, adhesives, and surface finishes used in the production of IC substrates.</p></p>
