<p><p>The mountain bike fork market is comprised of two main types: air spring suspension forks and coil spring suspension forks. Air spring suspension forks, as the name suggests, use compressed air to provide the spring-like effect. They are often lighter and offer more adjustability options for riders. On the other hand, coil spring suspension forks use metal coils to absorb shocks and provide suspension. They tend to be more durable and require less maintenance. Both types cater to different riders' needs and preferences, offering a variety of options in the mountain bike market.</p></p>
