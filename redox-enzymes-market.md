<p><p>Redox enzymes are classified into various types in the market. Catalase is an enzyme that helps in the breakdown of hydrogen peroxide, protecting cells from its harmful effects. Glucose oxidase is responsible for the oxidation of glucose, used in various applications like glucose monitoring systems. Laccase is an enzyme that aids in the oxidation of phenols or aromatic compounds, finding applications in bioremediation, biofuel cells, and waste treatment. Lastly, "others" category includes various redox enzymes with diverse functions, such as peroxidases or oxidases, used in different industries like food processing, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
