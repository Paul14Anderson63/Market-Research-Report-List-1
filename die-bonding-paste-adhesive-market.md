<p><p>Die bonding paste adhesive market types include conductive and non-conductive adhesives. Conductive adhesives are formulated with conductive fillers, such as silver, graphite, or copper, which enable the transfer of electrical signals and provide electrical connectivity between components. Non-conductive adhesives, on the other hand, do not contain conductive fillers and are used to bond components without interfering with electrical conductivity. Both types find applications in semiconductor packaging, electronics assembly, and other industries where precise and reliable bonding of microelectronic components is required.</p></p>
