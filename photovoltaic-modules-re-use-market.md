<p><p>Photovoltaic (PV) modules re-use market types can be classified into three categories: Monocrystalline Photovoltaic Modules, Polycrystalline Photovoltaic Modules, and Thin-film Modules. Monocrystalline PV modules are made from a single crystal structure, offering high efficiency and better performance in smaller spaces. Polycrystalline PV modules are made from multiple crystal fragments, providing a slightly lower efficiency but at a lower cost. Thin-film modules use a thin semiconductor layer, allowing for flexibility and low production costs. These different types cater to varying consumer needs and preferences in the PV re-use market.</p></p>
