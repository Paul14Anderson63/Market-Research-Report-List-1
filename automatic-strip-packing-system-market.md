<p><ul><li>Fully Automatic</li><li>Semi Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic strip packing system market can be categorized into two types: fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic systems involve the complete automation of the packing process, from feeding the strips to sealing the packs, requiring minimal human intervention. These systems are ideal for high-volume production lines that prioritize efficiency. On the other hand, semi-automatic systems require some level of human involvement in tasks like feeding the strips or sealing the packs. They are suitable for lower-volume production lines or those with varying pack sizes that require manual adjustments.</p></p>
