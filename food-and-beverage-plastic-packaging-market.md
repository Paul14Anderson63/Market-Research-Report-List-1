<p><p>The food and beverage plastic packaging market can be classified into two types: flexible packaging and rigid packaging. Flexible packaging includes materials like film, pouches, and bags that can be easily bent or folded. It offers benefits like high barrier properties, convenience, and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, rigid packaging involves materials like bottles, jars, and trays which are more rigid and sturdy. Rigid packaging provides durability, protection, and better visibility of the products. Both types of packaging play an essential role in preserving the quality and extending the shelf life of food and beverage products.</p></p>
