<p><p>The automotive electric cable market consists of two main types: PVC type and cross-linked type. PVC type cables are made of polyvinyl chloride, a flexible and durable material. They are commonly used in automotive applications due to their cost-effectiveness and easy installation. On the other hand, cross-linked cables are made with materials that have been treated with a cross-linking process, such as irradiation or chemical methods. This type of cable offers improved resistance to heat, abrasion, and chemicals, making them suitable for demanding automotive environments.</p></p>
