<p><strong>The Inorganic Conductive Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Curing Reaction Type</li><li>Hot Melt</li><li>High Temperature Sintering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the inorganic conductive adhesive market, there are three main types: curing reaction type, hot melt, and high-temperature sintering. Curing reaction type adhesives rely on a chemical reaction to harden and achieve conductivity. Hot melt adhesives are thermoplastic materials that melt when heated and solidify upon cooling to form a conductive bond. High-temperature sintering adhesives use high heat to fuse conductive particles together, creating a strong and conductive bond. These different types offer various advantages and are used in different applications within the electronics industry.</p></p>
