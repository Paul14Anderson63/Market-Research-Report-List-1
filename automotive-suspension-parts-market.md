Struts are typically used in the front suspension system and act as a shock absorber and structural support. Springs provide support and help absorb shocks, maintaining stability and controlling the vehicle's height. Bushings help reduce vibrations and provide cushioning between various suspension parts. The "others" category includes additional components like control arms, stabilizer bars, and ball joints that play crucial roles in enhancing vehicle handling, comfort, and safety.</p></p>
