<p><p>The automotive adaptive headlight market consists of three types: halogen headlights, xenon headlights, and LED headlights. Halogen headlights are the most common and affordable option, providing decent visibility but with limited brightness and lifespan. Xenon headlights offer a brighter and longer-lasting alternative, as they use xenon gas to produce a more intense light. Lastly, LED headlights are the most advanced option, offering superior brightness, energy efficiency, and longevity. They utilize light-emitting diodes to generate a sharp and focused beam, enhancing visibility and safety on the road.</p></p>
