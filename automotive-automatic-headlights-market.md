<p><p>The automotive automatic headlights market is comprised of two main types: Adaptive Front Lighting Headlights (AFS) and Adaptive Driving Beam Headlights (ADB). AFS headlights automatically adjust the light beam according to the vehicle's steering angle and speed, ensuring better visibility while cornering. ADB headlights use advanced sensors and cameras to detect oncoming vehicles and automatically dim the high beams to avoid dazzling other drivers while maintaining optimal lighting conditions. Both types aim to enhance safety and provide improved visibility for drivers in various driving conditions.</p></p>
