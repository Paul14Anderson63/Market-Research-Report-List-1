<p><p>The finished lubricant market encompasses various types of lubricants used for different applications. Mineral oil-based lubricants are derived from crude oil and are commonly used in automotive and industrial sectors. Synthetic oils, on the other hand, are artificially created lubricants that offer enhanced performance and longevity. Solid lubricants, such as graphite or molybdenum disulfide, provide lubrication in extreme conditions or where liquid lubricants are not suitable. Aqueous lubrication involves the use of water-based lubricants for environmentally friendly applications. Biolubricants are made from renewable sources and are biodegradable, making them an eco-friendly alternative to traditional lubricants.</p></p>
