<p><p>The High Barrier Metallized Film market is categorized into four major types: Polyvinylidene Chloride (PVDC), Ethylene-vinyl Alcohol Copolymer (EVOH), Polyamide (PA), and Polyester (PET, PEN). PVDC films offer excellent moisture and oxygen barrier properties, making them ideal for food packaging. EVOH films possess superior gas barrier properties, reducing the permeability of oxygen and other gases. PA films provide excellent mechanical strength and gas barrier properties, suitable for various applications. Polyester films are highly versatile, offering good barrier properties, transparency, and resistance to heat and chemicals, making them suitable for flexible packaging.</p></p>
