<p><p>The round flexible cable market consists of two main types: stranded cable and bundled/braided cable. Stranded cable is made up of several smaller strands of wires twisted together, allowing for more flexibility and durability. This type of cable is commonly used in applications where frequent bending and movement are required. Bundled/braided cable, on the other hand, consists of multiple insulated wires that are bundled or braided together. This type of cable provides extra protection against wear and tear, making it suitable for rugged environments.</p></p>
