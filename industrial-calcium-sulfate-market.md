<p><p>The industrial calcium sulfate market is categorized into two types: powder and lump. Powdered calcium sulfate is finely ground and is commonly used in various industries, such as construction, food, and pharmaceuticals. It is preferred for applications that require fast and uniform dispersion. On the other hand, lump calcium sulfate refers to larger pieces or chunks of the mineral. This type is often used in industries where slower dissolution rates are desired, like in certain chemical processes or in the manufacturing of plaster products.</p></p>
