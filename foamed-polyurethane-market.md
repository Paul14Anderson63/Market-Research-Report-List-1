Flexible Foam is used in applications like furniture, bedding, and automotive seating due to its comfort and durability. Spray Foam is employed for insulation purposes, forming a seamless and airtight barrier. Rigid Foam, on the other hand, is utilized in construction for its strength and energy efficiency.
