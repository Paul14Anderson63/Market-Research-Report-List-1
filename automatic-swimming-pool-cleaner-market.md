<p><p>The Automatic Swimming Pool Cleaner market can be categorized into two main types: Crawler Drive and Wheel Drive. Crawler Drive cleaners typically utilize tracks or belts to maneuver around the pool, allowing them to clean larger areas efficiently. On the other hand, Wheel Drive cleaners use wheels to move, making them more suitable for smaller pools or areas with obstacles. Both types offer automatic and convenient cleaning solutions, but their drive mechanisms differ, catering to different pool sizes and layouts.</p></p>
