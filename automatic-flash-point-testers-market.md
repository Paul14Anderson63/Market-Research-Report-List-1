<p><p>The Automatic Flash Point Testers market includes two main types: Open Cup Flash Point Tester and Closed Cup Flash Point Tester. The Open Cup Flash Point Tester is a device used to determine the lowest temperature at which a sample's vapors ignite when exposed to an open flame, providing vital information about safety precautions. On the other hand, a Closed Cup Flash Point Tester measures the flash point by simulating a closed environment, offering more accurate results, especially for volatile liquids. Overall, both types serve as reliable tools for assessing the flammability of substances.</p></p>
