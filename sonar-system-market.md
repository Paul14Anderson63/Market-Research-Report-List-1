<p><p>The sonar system market encompasses various types of systems that use sound waves to detect and locate underwater objects. Single-beam scanning sonar systems emit a single sound wave in one direction to create a 2D image of the sea floor. Multi-beam sonar systems emit multiple sound beams simultaneously to generate a more detailed 3D image. Side-scan sonar systems emit sound waves sideways and create detailed images of objects on the sea floor. Additionally, there are other types of sonar systems available in the market, each offering unique features and applications for underwater exploration and navigation.</p></p>
