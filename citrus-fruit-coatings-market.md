<p><p>Citrus Fruit Coatings market is classified into three types: wax, shellac, and others. Wax coatings, typically made of natural or synthetic waxes, are widely used due to their ability to enhance appearance and improve shelf life. Shellac coatings, derived from the resin secreted by insects, provide a durable and glossy finish. Other coatings include edible films, resin coatings, and composite coatings, which offer distinct benefits such as antibacterial properties, increased fruit firmness, or reduced moisture loss. These coatings play a vital role in maintaining fruit quality, preventing decay, and extending storage life.</p></p>
