<p><p>Diammonium phosphate (DAP) fertilizer market types include crystalline and granular forms. Crystalline DAP fertilizer is produced by the reaction of ammonia and phosphoric acid, resulting in a solid crystal structure that is typically small in size. Granular DAP fertilizer, on the other hand, is a larger-sized particle form of DAP that is created by combining fine DAP particles with binders and additives to form bigger granules. Both forms of DAP fertilizer provide essential nutrients like nitrogen and phosphorus to support plant growth and increase crop yields.</p></p>
