<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The sanding accessories market includes two main types: bonded and coated. Bonded sanding accessories are made by adhering abrasive grains to a backing material using a resin or adhesive. This type of accessory is often used for heavy-duty applications and provides durability and consistency. Coated sanding accessories, on the other hand, have abrasive grains attached to their surface through a coating process. They are typically used for finer and more precise sanding tasks, providing better control and finishing results. Both types cater to different sanding needs in various industries.</p></p>
