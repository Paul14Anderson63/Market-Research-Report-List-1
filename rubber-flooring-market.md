<p><p>Rubber flooring can be classified into two types: natural rubber and synthetic rubber. Natural rubber is derived from the sap of rubber trees and is known for its high elasticity and durability. It is commonly used in applications where resilience and resistance to wear are important, such as gyms, playgrounds, and sports facilities. On the other hand, synthetic rubber is artificially produced and offers a similar level of performance. It is often preferred in industrial settings due to its greater chemical resistance and cost-effectiveness. Both types of rubber flooring serve as reliable and versatile options for various flooring needs.</p></p>
