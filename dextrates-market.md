<p><p>Dextrates are a form of carbohydrates used in various industries, including pharmaceuticals and food production. There are two main market types for dextrates: anhydrous and hydrous. Anhydrous dextrates are crystalline powders that have been dried to remove all water content, making them more stable and suitable for certain applications. Hydrous dextrates, on the other hand, contain moisture and are typically in a granular form, which may be advantageous for specific manufacturing processes.
