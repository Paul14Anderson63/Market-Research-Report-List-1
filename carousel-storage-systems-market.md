Horizontal carousel storage systems consist of a series of horizontally rotating shelves that move items to picking stations, reducing travel time and increasing productivity. Vertical carousel storage systems, on the other hand, feature a series of vertically rotating shelves that bring items to the operator at an ergonomic height, minimizing bending and reaching. Both types of carousel storage systems offer space-saving solutions and enhance workflow efficiency in various industries.</p></p>
