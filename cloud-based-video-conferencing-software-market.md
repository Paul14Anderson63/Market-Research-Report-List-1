<p><strong>The Cloud Based Video Conferencing Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SVC Architecture</li><li>AVC Architecture</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cloud-based video conferencing software market types refer to the architectural designs used to deliver video conferencing services over the internet. SVC (Scalable Video Coding) architecture is based on breaking the video data into different layers, allowing efficient streaming and adaptability to network fluctuations, ensuring a smooth video experience. AVC (Advanced Video Coding) architecture uses a fixed video layer, resulting in higher quality video but may be less adaptable to network conditions. These market types offer different trade-offs, with SVC prioritizing adaptability and AVC focusing on video quality.</p></p>
