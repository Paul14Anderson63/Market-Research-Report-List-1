<p><p>The electric pallet truck market includes two types of trucks: pallet transporting trucks and pallet stacking trucks. Pallet transporting trucks are designed to efficiently move pallets from one location to another within a facility. They are equipped with a platform or forks that can hold and transport the pallets. Pallet stacking trucks, on the other hand, are used to both transport and stack pallets on top of each other. These trucks typically have a higher lift capacity and are often used in warehouses and distribution centers.</p></p>
