<p><ul><li>Slotted Wire Ducts</li><li>Solid Wall Wire Ducts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wiring duct market can be divided into two main types: slotted wire ducts and solid wall wire ducts. Slotted wire ducts are designed with openings or slots along their length, allowing for easy insertion and removal of wires and cables. They provide better ventilation and flexibility for cable management. On the other hand, solid wall wire ducts have a solid continuous construction, which offers more protection and security for the wires. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in organizing and protecting electrical wiring systems.</p></p>
