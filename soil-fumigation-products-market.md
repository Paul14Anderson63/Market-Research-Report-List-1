<p><p>Soil fumigation products market types refer to different chemicals used to treat soil before planting crops. 1,3-Dichloropropene, Chloropicrin, Methyl Bromide, Metam Sodium, Phosphine, and Others are the main types. 1,3-Dichloropropene is a liquid fumigant effective against plant-parasitic nematodes. Chloropicrin is a versatile fumigant used for broad-spectrum control of weeds, pests, and pathogens. Methyl Bromide is a highly effective fumigant but is being phased out due to its ozone-depleting properties. Metam Sodium is used for soil-borne pest control and disease suppression. Phosphine is a gas fumigant used for its broad-spectrum activity. "Others" refer to alternative or lesser-used soil fumigant products in the market.</p></p>
