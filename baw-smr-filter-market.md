<p><p>The BAW (Bulk Acoustic Wave) SMR (Surface Mount Resonator) Filter market offers various types of filters. Lattice Type Filter employs a periodic arrangement of resonators to achieve filtering. Stacked Crystal Filter involves stacking multiple crystals with different resonant frequencies to achieve a narrower bandwidth. Coupled Resonator Filter exploits the interaction between multiple resonators coupled together to attain desired filter characteristics. These filter types cater to different applications, such as wireless communication, ensuring efficient signal filtering.</p></p>
