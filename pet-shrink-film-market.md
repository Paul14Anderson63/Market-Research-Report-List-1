<p><p>PET shrink film is a type of packaging material that is widely used in various industries. It is classified into three types based on its shrink capabilities. Low shrink film offers minimal shrinkage, usually around 30%, making it suitable for products with irregular shapes or sizes. Medium shrink film, with a shrink rate of about 35-50%, is commonly used for packaging bottles, cans, or boxes. High shrink film, with a shrink rate of over 50%, is ideal for tightly wrapping products with smooth surfaces, such as DVDs, electronics, or pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
