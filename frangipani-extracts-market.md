<p><p>Frangipani extracts are available in two different market types: organic extract and conventional extract. Organic extracts are derived from frangipani flowers that have been grown and processed without the use of synthetic pesticides or fertilizers. They are produced following strict organic farming practices and are often considered to be more natural and environmentally friendly. On the other hand, conventional extracts are derived from frangipani flowers that may have been exposed to synthetic chemicals during cultivation. These extracts are more widely available and may cater to consumers who prioritize affordability over organic credentials.</p></p>
