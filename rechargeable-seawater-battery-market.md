<p><p>The Rechargeable Seawater Battery market comprises two types: Natural Sea Water Battery and Blended Sea Water Battery. Natural Sea Water Battery uses seawater directly as an electrolyte, eliminating the need for salt and freshwater. It is a more environmentally friendly option that offers a longer lifespan and higher energy density. On the other hand, Blended Sea Water Battery combines sea and freshwater electrolytes, providing a balance between cost-effectiveness and performance. Both types contribute to the development of sustainable energy storage solutions using readily available seawater resources.</p></p>
