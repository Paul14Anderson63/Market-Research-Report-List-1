<p><strong>The Electric Inflatable Boats Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Center Console</li><li>Side Console</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric inflatable boats are available in two main market types: center console and side console. In a center console configuration, the steering station is located in the center of the boat, allowing for easy access and visibility for the driver. This layout is popular for larger boats and provides ample space for passengers and storage. On the other hand, side console models have the steering station positioned on one side of the boat, maximizing deck space and providing a more compact design. Side console boats are often favored for smaller and more agile vessels.</p></p>
