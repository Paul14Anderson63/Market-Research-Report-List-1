<p><p>The electric vehicle busbars market consists of three main types: copper busbars, aluminium busbars, and flexible busbars. Copper busbars are made of copper and offer excellent conductivity and low resistance, making them suitable for high-power applications. Aluminium busbars are made of aluminium and are lightweight, cost-effective, and provide sufficient conductivity for most electric vehicle applications. Flexible busbars are made of copper or aluminum, but they have a special design that allows for flexibility, making them suitable for applications with limited space or complex layouts.</p></p>
