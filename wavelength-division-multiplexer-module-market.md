Dense Wavelength Division Multiplexer (DWDM) is designed for long-haul transmissions, enabling up to hundreds of different wavelengths. Both CWDM and DWDM are widely used in telecommunications, data centers, and other industries to optimize fiber optic network capacity, minimize costs, and enhance efficiency.</p></p>
