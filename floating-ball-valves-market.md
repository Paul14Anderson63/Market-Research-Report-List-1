<p><p>Floating ball valves are a type of quarter-turn valve that uses a floating ball to control and regulate the flow of liquids or gases through a pipeline. The ball is held in place by two seat rings, allowing it to move freely in response to changes in pressure or flow. These valves are commonly used in industries such as oil and gas, water and wastewater treatment, and power generation, due to their reliable performance and ease of maintenance.</p><p>The future outlook for the floating ball valves market appears promising.
