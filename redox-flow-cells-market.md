<p><p>Redox flow cells, also known as redox flow batteries, are a type of rechargeable batteries that store and release electrical energy by means of reversible oxidation and reduction reactions. Unlike conventional batteries that store energy in the electrode material, redox flow cells store energy in liquid electrolytes contained in external tanks. This unique design allows for flexible scalability and longer storage durations, making them ideal for stationary energy storage applications.</p><p>The future outlook for the redox flow cells market is promising. The increasing demand for renewable energy sources and the need for energy storage solutions to ensure a stable and reliable power supply are driving the growth of this market. Redox flow cells are particularly suitable for large-scale energy storage, such as grid-level applications, due to their scalability and long-lasting performance.</p><p>Moreover, the rising adoption of electric vehicles and the need for efficient energy management systems further contribute to the growth of the redox flow cells market.
