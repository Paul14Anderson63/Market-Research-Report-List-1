<p><p>The linear filling capping machines market can be categorized into two types: fully automatic and semiautomatic. Fully automatic machines perform the filling and capping processes without any human intervention, using advanced control systems and sensors. They offer high efficiency and minimal errors. Semiautomatic machines, on the other hand, require some level of human involvement throughout the filling and capping processes. While they offer lower productivity compared to fully automatic machines, they are generally more affordable and suitable for smaller production volumes. Both machine types play a crucial role in the packaging industry, satisfying different production needs.</p></p>
