<p><p>The light vehicle wiring market can be categorized into three types: Copper Core, Aluminum Core, and Others. Copper Core wiring refers to cables made of copper, which is a highly conductive material known for its excellent electrical properties. Aluminum Core wiring, on the other hand, utilizes aluminum as the conductive material, which is cheaper but less conductive than copper. The "Others" category includes alternative wiring materials such as hybrid or composite cores that combine different materials to achieve desired electrical performance and cost-efficiency in the light vehicle wiring market.</p></p>
