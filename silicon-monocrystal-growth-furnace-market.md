<p><strong>The Silicon Monocrystal Growth Furnace Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Czochralski (CZ) Method Furnace</li><li>Floating Zone (FZ) Method Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Silicon Monocrystal Growth Furnace market consists of two main types: Czochralski (CZ) Method Furnace and Floating Zone (FZ) Method Furnace. The CZ Method Furnace utilizes a crucible containing silicon feedstock, which is melted and slowly pulled upwards to form a monocrystalline structure. On the other hand, the FZ Method Furnace uses an induction coil to generate localized melting and solidification in a controlled atmosphere, producing a high-quality monocrystal. Both methods play crucial roles in the production of silicon wafers used in various electronic devices.</p></p>
