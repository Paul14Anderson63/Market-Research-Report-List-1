<p><p>An electromechanical dryer, also known as an electric dryer, is a household appliance used for drying clothes. <p><p>Electromechanical dryer market comprises two main types, namely electro dryers and mechanical dryers. Electro dryers utilize electrical energy to generate heat, which is then used to dry clothes. These dryers are more energy-efficient and offer advanced features such as customizable drying programs and sensors. On the other hand, mechanical dryers rely on mechanical actions like spinning and warm air circulation to dry clothes. They are generally less expensive but may consume more energy. The choice between these dryer types depends on individual preferences, budget, and desired level of technological advancements.</p></p>
