Acid concentrates are used to acidify the water used to prepare dialysate, while bicarbonate concentrates are used to buffer or alkalize the dialysate. Modifiers for haemodialysis concentrates are added to enhance dialysis efficiency or to overcome specific patient needs. These different types of concentrates are crucial in maintaining the balance of electrolytes and pH during the dialysis process.</p></p>
