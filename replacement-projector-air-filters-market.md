<p><p>The replacement projector air filters market comprises two main types: unwoven fabric filters and metal filters. Unwoven fabric filters are made from synthetic fibers that are fused together to form a filter media. These filters are usually disposable and offer efficient air filtration. On the other hand, metal filters are made of metal mesh material that can be cleaned and reused. These filters are known for their durability and long lifespan. Both types of filters play a crucial role in maintaining the air quality and efficient functioning of projectors.</p></p>
