<p><p>Sulfamate Nickel Plating is a widely used electroplating process for surface finishing, offering excellent corrosion resistance and wear properties. The market can be categorized into two types: chloride and non-chloride. Chloride sulfamate nickel plating involves the addition of chloride ions to the plating bath, which enhances the plating speed but requires careful control as it can affect the stability of the bath. On the other hand, non-chloride sulfamate nickel plating does not use chloride ions, providing a more stable and controlled process, but with a slightly lower plating speed.</p></p>
