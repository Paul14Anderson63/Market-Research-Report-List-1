<p><p>The plastics used in household appliances can be categorized into various types. ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) is commonly used for its impact resistance and easy processability. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is often favored for its flame retardant properties. POM (Polyoxymethylene) is known for its mechanical strength and low friction characteristics. PRT (Polyester) is valued for its excellent electrical insulation properties. PBT (Polybutylene Terephthalate) is chosen for its heat resistance and dimensional stability. Lastly, the "Others" category encompasses a range of plastic materials employed in household appliances.</p></p>
