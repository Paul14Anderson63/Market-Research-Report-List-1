<p><p>The commercial aircraft turbine blades and vanes market consists of three main types: Low Pressure Turbine (LPT) Blades and Vanes, Intermediate Pressure Turbine (IPT) Blades and Vanes, and High Pressure Turbine (HPT) Blades and Vanes. LPT blades and vanes are situated at the rear section of the turbine and are responsible for extracting energy from the exhaust gases. IPT blades and vanes are placed between the LPT and HPT and play a crucial role in generating power. HPT blades and vanes are located at the front of the turbine and are responsible for extracting energy from the combustion gases. Each type of blade and vane serves a specific purpose in the overall functioning of the commercial aircraft turbine.</p></p>
