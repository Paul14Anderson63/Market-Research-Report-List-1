<p><p>Packaged salt market consists of different types such as Rock Salt, Solar Salt, and Evap Salt. Rock Salt is obtained from underground salt deposits and is commonly used for de-icing roads and as a table salt substitute. Solar Salt is produced through the evaporation of seawater or saltwater lakes and is widely used for industrial purposes. Evap Salt is made by evaporating brine or saltwater and is commonly used for water softening and in swimming pools. These different types of packaged salt cater to various industries and consumer needs.</p></p>
