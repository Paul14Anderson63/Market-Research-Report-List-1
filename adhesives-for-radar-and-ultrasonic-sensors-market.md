<p><p>Adhesives for the radar and ultrasonic sensors market can be classified into three types: liquid, solid, and paste. Liquid adhesives are fluid substances that can be applied easily and provide a strong bond when cured. Solid adhesives, on the other hand, are in solid form and require heating or melting to be applied. They create a robust bond once cooled or hardened. Paste adhesives have a viscous consistency and can be easily spread on surfaces. They offer good adhesion properties and are commonly used for sensitive electronic components.</p></p>
