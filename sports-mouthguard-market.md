<p><ul><li>Preformed Mouthguard</li><li>Thermoformed Mouthguard</li><li>Custom Mouthguard</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sports mouthguard market offers three main types of products: preformed mouthguards, thermoformed mouthguards, and custom mouthguards. Preformed mouthguards are ready-to-use, made from a standard shape that can be adjusted slightly to fit the user's mouth. Thermoformed mouthguards are made from a mold using heat and pressure, allowing for a more customized fit. Custom mouthguards are individually designed based on the user's dental impressions, offering the highest level of customization and protection. These different types cater to individuals with varying needs and preferences in the sports mouthguard market.</p></p>
