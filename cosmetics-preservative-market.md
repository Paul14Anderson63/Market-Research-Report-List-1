<p><p>Cosmetics preservative market types can be categorized into four main categories. Phenol derivatives are preservatives that are derived from phenol compounds and are used to prevent the growth of bacteria and other harmful microorganisms in cosmetics. Formaldehyde-based preservatives, as the name suggests, contain formaldehyde and are effective against a wide range of microorganisms. Organic acids, such as benzoic acid and sorbic acid, are commonly used natural preservatives in cosmetics. Lastly, there are other preservatives available that do not fall into these specific categories but are still used to improve the shelf life and safety of cosmetic products.</p></p>
