<p><p>Laundry balls come in various market types, characterized by their shape. Disk shape laundry balls are flat, round devices with raised bumps or grooves. They are designed to agitate and clean clothes in the washing machine. Sphere shape laundry balls are round, resembling small balls or marbles. They aid in cleaning by creating turbulence and lifting dirt particles from clothes. Doughnut shape laundry balls are circular with a hole in the center. They generate a vortex-like motion to efficiently remove dirt and stains from garments. These different market types cater to diverse consumer preferences and laundry needs.</p></p>
