<p><p>Recycling carbon fiber is done through two market types: Chopped Recycling Carbon Fiber and Milled Recycling Carbon Fiber. Chopped recycling carbon fiber involves cutting carbon fiber materials into small pieces, which can then be used in various applications like thermoplastic compounds or concrete reinforcement. Milled recycling carbon fiber, on the other hand, involves grinding carbon fiber materials into fine particles that can be utilized in manufacturing processes such as injection molding or 3D printing. Both methods contribute to the sustainability of carbon fiber by repurposing waste materials and reducing environmental impact.</p></p>
