With the increasing number of cyber threats and data breaches, businesses and individuals are realizing the importance of robust security measures. <p><p>Multi-factor authentication technology market is categorized into different types based on the number of factors used for verification. Two-factor authentication (2FA) requires two forms of authentication, such as a password and a fingerprint. Three-factor authentication (3FA) adds another layer, like a security token. Four-factor authentication (4FA) incorporates an additional factor like a voice recognition or facial recognition. Five-factor authentication (5FA) goes a step further by including a behavioral or biometric factor like keystroke dynamics or iris scanning. These types offer increasing levels of security and protection against unauthorized access or fraudulent activities.</p></p>
