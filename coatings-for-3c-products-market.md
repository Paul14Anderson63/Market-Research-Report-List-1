<p><p>Coatings for 3C products, such as smartphones, tablets, and laptops, can be divided into two market types: UV curing coating and thermal curing coating. UV curing coatings are applied to the surface of these products and are instantly cured by exposure to ultraviolet light, providing a protective and scratch-resistant layer. On the other hand, thermal curing coatings require heat for the curing process, offering a durable and high-quality protective finish. Both types of coatings enhance the appearance and resilience of 3C products, ensuring their longevity and performance.</p></p>
