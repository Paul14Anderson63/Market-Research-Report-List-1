<p><p>Ethyl Polysilicate, also known as ethyl silicate, is a compound made from the reaction of silicon tetrachloride with ethanol. It has excellent properties like chemical resistance, high adhesion, and fast curing, making it a preferred choice in many applications.</p><p>The ethyl polysilicate market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue to grow at a CAGR of 4.1% during the forecast period. One of the major driving factors for this growth is the increasing demand for ethyl polysilicate in the construction industry due to its excellent binding properties in coatings and adhesives. The rise in construction activities, especially in developing countries, is fueling the demand for ethyl polysilicate.</p><p>Moreover, the growing awareness about the benefits of using ethyl polysilicate in paints and coatings for protection against corrosion and weathering is further propelling market growth.
