<p><p>The automotive drivetrain lubricant market consists of two main types: fully synthetic and semisynthetic lubricants. Fully synthetic lubricants are manufactured using a complex chemical process and offer excellent performance and protection for the drivetrain components. They are more expensive but offer a longer service life and enhanced fuel efficiency. On the other hand, semisynthetic lubricants are a blend of synthetic and conventional base oils, providing a balance between performance and cost. They offer good protection and durability but at a more affordable price point.</p></p>
