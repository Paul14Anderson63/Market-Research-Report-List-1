There are two types of photosensitive polyimide coatings - positive and negative. Positive photosensitive polyimide allows the development of areas exposed to light, creating a patterned coating. On the other hand, negative photosensitive polyimide coatings allow the development of areas that were not exposed to light. Both types provide precise and durable coatings, making them suitable for applications in electronics, aerospace, automotive, and other industries.</p></p>
