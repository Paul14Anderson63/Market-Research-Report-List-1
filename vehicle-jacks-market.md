<p><p>Vehicle jacks can be classified into four main types: manual, pneumatic, electric, and hydraulic jacks. Manual jacks are operated manually by cranking or using a lever. Pneumatic jacks use compressed air to lift vehicles, while electric jacks employ an electric motor for lifting. Hydraulic jacks use hydraulic fluid to raise and lower vehicles. Each type has its own advantages and suitability for different applications. These jacks are widely used in the automotive industry and are essential for tasks such as changing tires or performing maintenance.</p></p>
