<p><strong>The Automotive TPMS (Tire-Pressure Monitoring System) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct TPMS</li><li>Indirect TPMS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive TPMS (Tire-Pressure Monitoring System) is a technology used in vehicles to monitor the air pressure of tires in real-time. There are two types of TPMS systems: direct TPMS and indirect TPMS. </p><p>Direct TPMS uses sensors mounted inside the tires that send tire pressure data to the vehicle's control unit. This system provides accurate and precise readings but requires sensor installation.</p><p>Indirect TPMS, on the other hand, utilizes the Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) to estimate tire pressure based on wheel speed sensors. This system is less accurate but does not require additional sensors, making it a cost-effective option.</p></p>
