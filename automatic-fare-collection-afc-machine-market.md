Fare Gates are access control devices that allow only passengers with valid tickets to enter or exit specific areas, ensuring proper fare collection. These machines play a crucial role in facilitating efficient and automated fare collection systems in various transportation systems, contributing to seamless travel experiences for passengers.</p></p>
