Cold flow improvers prevent fuel gelling at low temperatures, and detergents and dispersants keep fuel systems clean. Octane and cetane improvers enhance fuel combustion quality. Dyes and markers help differentiate biofuels from conventional fuels. Lastly, there are other additives with various functions, such as stabilizers, lubricity enhancers, and biocides, which further improve biofuel quality and usability.</p></p>
