<p><p>The coconut hair oil market can be divided into two main types: fresh coconut squeeze and copra press market. In the fresh coconut squeeze market, the oil is extracted from freshly shredded coconut meat through a mechanical process, ensuring high quality and natural aroma. On the other hand, the copra press market involves extracting oil from dried coconut meat, known as copra, which is achieved through a mechanical pressing method. While both types offer coconut hair oil, fresh coconut squeeze market is known for its superior quality due to the freshness of the coconut used in the process.</p></p>
