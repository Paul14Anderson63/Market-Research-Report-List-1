<p><p>The motorized decoiler machine market offers three types of machines - single, dual, and multi-head. The single head decoiler machine has only one reel, which allows for the unwinding of one coil at a time. The dual head decoiler machine has two reels, enabling the simultaneous unwinding of two coils. Lastly, the multi-head decoiler machine includes more than two reels, providing the capability to unwind multiple coils concurrently. These different types of machines cater to various production requirements, allowing for increased efficiency and output in different industrial settings.</p></p>
