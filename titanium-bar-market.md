<p><p>The titanium bar market can be categorized into different types based on the titanium grade. TA1, TA2, and TA3 are widely recognized titanium grades. TA1 is a commercially pure titanium grade known for its excellent formability and corrosion resistance. TA2 is another commercially pure grade, but with improved strength. TA3, on the other hand, contains small amounts of aluminum and vanadium, making it even stronger than TA2. Finally, there are other titanium grades available in the market that possess unique properties suited for specific applications.</p></p>
