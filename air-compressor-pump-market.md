<p><p>The Air Compressor Pump Market is categorized into two types: Positive Displacement Compression Pump and Speed Type Compression Pump. </p><p>Positive Displacement Compression Pump functions by trapping air in a chamber and decreasing its volume to increase pressure. This type is ideal for applications requiring high pressure or steady flow rates. </p><p>On the other hand, Speed Type Compression Pump relies on a rotating impeller to create airflow and pressure. It is suitable for applications where high volume and continuous airflow are necessary. This type is commonly used in large-scale operations.</p></p>
