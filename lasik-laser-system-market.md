<p><p>The LASIK Laser System market consists of various types, including spot-scanning lasers, slit-scanning lasers, and wavefront-guided lasers. Spot-scanning lasers utilize a small spot of laser energy to shape the cornea, ensuring precise corrections. Slit-scanning lasers use a narrow, slit-shaped beam to create a pattern on the cornea, allowing for a custom treatment. Wavefront-guided lasers analyze the eye's unique imperfections and guide the laser to correct them. These different types cater to diverse patient needs, providing precise and personalized treatments for vision correction.</p></p>
