<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>There are several types of Stevia in the market today. The Reb-A series refers to Stevia extracts that contain a high concentration of the sweet compound Reb-A, which provides a strong sweetness with minimal bitterness. The STV series refers to Stevia extracts that have a balanced sweetness while maintaining low bitterness. Glucosyl Stevia is a type of Stevia extract that has its sweetness enhanced by attaching glucose molecules to the Reb-A compound. Reb M and Reb D are two compounds extracted from Stevia that offer a sweeter taste compared to Reb-A.</p></p>
