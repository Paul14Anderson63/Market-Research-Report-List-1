These magnets offer several advantages over conventional magnets, such as higher field strength and improved image quality. The demand for superconducting magnets is increasing due to their ability to provide better diagnostic accuracy and reduce scan time.</p><p>Another trend in the market is the growing preference for open MRI magnets. <p><p>The MRI magnets market is categorized into three main types: 1.5T, 3.0T, and Others.
