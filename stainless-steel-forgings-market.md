<p><p>The stainless steel forgings market encompasses three main types: castings, hot/cold forged parts, and sintered parts. Castings involve pouring molten stainless steel into a mold to shape it. Hot forging involves heating the steel and shaping it with mechanical pressure, while cold forging involves shaping at room temperature. Sintering involves compacting fine stainless steel powder and heating it to form a solid piece. Each method has its own advantages and applications in various industries, such as automotive, aerospace, and construction.</p></p>
