<p><p>The automobile exterior rearview mirror market consists of two types: plane mirror and convex mirror. Plane mirrors provide a direct reflection, offering a clear image of the objects behind the vehicle. On the other hand, convex mirrors are curved, allowing for a wider field of view. Although they may distort the objects' images, they enable drivers to see a larger area and help in preventing blind spots. Both mirror types have their own advantages and are used in different situations based on the driver's preference and safety requirements.</p></p>
