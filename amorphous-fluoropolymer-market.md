<p><ul><li>Solid</li><li>Solution</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Amorphous fluoropolymers are a type of polymer material that has a non-crystalline or disordered molecular structure. They can be classified into two types: solid and solution market. </p><p>In the solid market, amorphous fluoropolymers are available in various forms such as pellets, powders, or granules. These solid materials can be easily melted and molded into different shapes or used as a coating for various applications, providing excellent chemical resistance and high-temperature stability.</p><p>In the solution market, amorphous fluoropolymers are dissolved in a solvent to form a liquid solution. This solution can be applied as a coating or impregnation by spray, dip, or spin coating methods. Once the solvent evaporates, a solid film with outstanding chemical resistance and weatherability is formed.</p></p>
