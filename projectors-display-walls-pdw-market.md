<p><p>Projectors & Display Walls (PDW) are audiovisual equipment used in various industries such as cinema, education, healthcare, and entertainment for displaying content and presentations on a large-scale. Laser projectors offer superior picture quality, color accuracy, and brightness compared to traditional lamp-based projectors. Additionally, with the increasing popularity of 4K and 8K resolution content, the market for high-resolution PDWs is expected to witness significant growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the demand for display walls is rising as organizations seek to enhance collaboration and communication. Display walls enable the simultaneous presentation of multiple visual content sources, improving productivity and decision-making processes in industries like corporate offices, control rooms, and command centers.
