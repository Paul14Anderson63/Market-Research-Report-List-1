<p><p>The electric vacuum pump control unit market is categorized into three types: diaphragm type, leaf type, and swing piston type. The diaphragm type utilizes a flexible diaphragm that moves back and forth, creating the vacuum pressure required. The leaf type employs flexible leaves or blades to generate the necessary vacuum. The swing piston type consists of a piston that swings back and forth within a cylinder, producing the required vacuum pressure. These various types offer different ways of creating vacuum pressure and can be used in different applications depending on their specific characteristics.</p></p>
