<p><p>The Automotive Yaw Rate Sensor market comprises two main types: the Piezoelectric Type and the Micromechanical Type. The Piezoelectric Type utilizes piezoelectric crystals to convert the yaw motion of the vehicle into an electrical signal. This type is known for its high sensitivity and accuracy. On the other hand, the Micromechanical Type utilizes microelectromechanical systems (MEMS) technology to measure the vehicle's yaw rate. It is characterized by its small size, cost-effectiveness, and low power consumption. Both types play a crucial role in vehicle safety systems, providing essential data for stability control and maneuverability.</p></p>
