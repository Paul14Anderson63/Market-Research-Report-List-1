<p><p>Flexitanks are specialized containers used to transport liquids, such as food-grade products, chemicals, and oils in bulk. There are three types of flexitanks in the market. Multilayer flexitanks consist of multiple layers of materials, offering enhanced strength and protection against leakage or puncture. Monolayer flexitanks have a single layer of material, suitable for non-hazardous products. Bilayer flexitanks comprise two layers, combining the benefits of both multilayer and monolayer flexitanks. Each type caters to different transportation needs, ensuring safe and efficient liquid delivery.</p></p>
