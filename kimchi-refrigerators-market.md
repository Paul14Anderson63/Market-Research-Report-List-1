<p><p>Kimchi refrigerators are specially designed refrigerators used for storing Korean traditional fermented vegetable dish called kimchi. These refrigerators also have features like air purification systems and carbon filters to prevent odor contamination. Some advanced models even offer features like UV sterilization and automatic fermentation control, which further enhance the quality and longevity of the kimchi.</p><p>The market analysis for kimchi refrigerators suggests that the demand for these specialized appliances is steadily increasing.
