<p><p>UV cured printing inks are a type of ink that are dried and cured using ultraviolet light, resulting in fast and efficient drying process. There are two main types of UV cured printing inks: arc curing and LED curing. Arc curing involves using a traditional mercury arc lamp to emit UV light, while LED curing uses light-emitting diodes to generate UV light. Both methods offer quick drying times, improved print quality, and are environmentally friendly due to their low volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions.</p></p>
