<p><p>The automobile tire market can be categorized into two main types: Bias Tire and Radial Tire. Bias tires are constructed with a crisscross ply pattern, where the plies cross each other at an angle, resulting in a flexible and durable tire. On the other hand, radial tires are designed with a radial ply structure, where the plies are placed radially from bead to bead, providing better handling, fuel efficiency, and a smoother ride. Both types have their specific advantages and are used in different types of vehicles and terrain conditions.</p></p>
