<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle Charging Pile market includes two types: AC Charging Pile and DC Charging Pile. AC Charging Pile refers to the charging equipment that converts AC power to DC power for charging electric vehicles. It is suitable for overnight charging or long-term parking scenarios. DC Charging Pile, on the other hand, provides direct DC power supply to electric vehicles, enabling fast and convenient charging. It is primarily used in public places or areas with high traffic flow. Both types of charging piles contribute to the development and adoption of electric vehicles.</p></p>
