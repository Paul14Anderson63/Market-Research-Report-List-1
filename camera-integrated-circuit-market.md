Memory Chips store image data and control information. Image Processors enhance image quality by performing tasks like noise reduction and image stabilization. IPCSoC (Image Processor and Communication System on Chip) offers advanced features like real-time image processing and wireless communication. Additionally, there are other markets that offer different types of camera ICs, such as lenses, sensors, and autofocus mechanisms. These chips enable the functioning of cameras in various electronic devices.</p></p>
