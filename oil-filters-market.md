<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The oil filters market offers various types of filters for different purposes. Mechanical oil filters primarily use a filter media to trap contaminants and prevent them from reaching the engine. Magnetic oil filters employ magnets to attract and remove metallic particles from the oil. Centrifugal oil filters work by spinning the oil rapidly, causing the heavier contaminants to separate out and settle at the bottom. These filters are designed to ensure cleaner oil flows through the engine, enhancing its performance and longevity.</p></p>
