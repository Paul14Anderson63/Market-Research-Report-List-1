<p><p>The feeding heating lamp market comprises two main types of lamps: infrared lamps and ceramics lamps. Infrared lamps emit radiant heat, which is absorbed by objects and provides consistent warmth. These lamps are used to provide a natural heat source for reptiles, poultry, and other animals. Ceramics lamps, on the other hand, generate heat through electrical resistance in ceramic materials. They are widely used in heating applications for various animals, including chicks and piglets. Both types of lamps are designed to efficiently and safely provide heating solutions for different animal rearing or breeding needs.</p></p>
