<p><p>The Personal Flotation Device (PFD) market consists of various types of PFDs. Buoyancy Aid (Foam Core) PFDs are designed to provide buoyancy and support for water activities. Life Jackets are specifically designed to turn an unconscious person face-up in the water. Survival Suits are full-body immersion suits that provide thermal protection and buoyancy in survival situations. Buoyancy Compensators are primarily used by scuba divers to control their buoyancy underwater. Other types of PFDs include inflatable PFDs, hybrid PFDs, and special-purpose PFDs for specific activities like kayaking or sailing.</p></p>
