<p><p>The IoT RFID Electronic Tags market is divided into two main types: passive RFID and active RFID. Passive RFID tags do not have their own power source and rely on energy from the RFID reader to operate. These tags are cost-effective and suitable for use in environments with short read ranges. On the other hand, active RFID tags come with their own power source, enabling them to transmit data over longer distances and allowing for real-time tracking capabilities. Although active tags are more expensive, they are ideal for applications that require continuous monitoring and higher accuracy.</p></p>
