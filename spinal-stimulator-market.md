<p><p>The spinal stimulator market comprises two main types: full embedding stimulators and semi-embedding stimulators. Full embedding stimulators are implanted entirely under the skin and have electrodes placed directly on the spinal cord. They offer more precise pain relief but require a more invasive surgery. On the other hand, semi-embedding stimulators are partially implanted, with some components remaining outside the body. They provide less precise pain relief but are less invasive, making them suitable for individuals who prefer a less complex surgery.</p></p>
