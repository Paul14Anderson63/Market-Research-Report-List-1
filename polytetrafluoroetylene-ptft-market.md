<p><p>Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) is a versatile fluoropolymer material used in various industries. It is available in different market types, including granular, fine powder, dispersion, and micronized. Granular PTFE consists of small particles and is commonly used for molding and extrusion applications. Fine powder PTFE has a smaller particle size and is suitable for coatings, lubricants, and additives. Dispersion PTFE is a liquid form used in coatings, while micronized PTFE has a very fine particle size and is commonly used in lubricants and high-performance applications where a smooth surface is desired. These different market types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
