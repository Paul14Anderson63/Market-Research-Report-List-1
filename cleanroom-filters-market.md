ULPA (Ultra-Low Penetration Air) filters provide even higher filtration efficiency for smaller particles. Other types of filters may include activated carbon filters, which remove gaseous contaminants, and molecular filters, which eliminate specific chemicals. These filters play a crucial role in preventing the contamination of sensitive areas by ensuring the air is free from harmful particles or pollutants.</p></p>
