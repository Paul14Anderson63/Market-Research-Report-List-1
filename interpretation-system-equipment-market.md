<p><p>The interpretation system equipment market consists of two types: wired and wireless. Wired systems utilize cables to transmit audio signals between the interpreter and listeners. This type of system ensures a stable and reliable connection, but limits mobility. On the other hand, wireless systems use wireless communication technologies to transmit audio signals, providing more flexibility and freedom for the interpreters to move around. However, wireless systems may be affected by signal interference or limitations in the range of coverage. Both types cater to different needs and environments in the interpretation industry.</p></p>
