Speciality fertilizers play a significant role in precision agriculture by providing targeted nutrient management and reducing wastage. Additionally, evolving farming practices, such as hydroponics and fertigation, are further fueling the demand for speciality fertilizers.</p><p>The current outlook of the speciality fertilizers market is promising.
