<p><p>The metallized plastic film capacitor market consists of two key types: AC film capacitors and DC film capacitors. AC film capacitors are designed to handle alternating currents and are commonly used in applications such as power supplies, motors, and amplifiers. They offer high capacitance and voltage ratings, as well as low dielectric losses. On the other hand, DC film capacitors are ideal for direct current applications, providing reliable performance in electronic devices like inverters, converters, and DC power supplies. They are known for their stability, high insulation resistance, and low self-inductance.</p></p>
